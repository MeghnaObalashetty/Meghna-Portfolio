About Me

I am a dedicated full-stack web developer with over 4+ years of experience in the software industry. My passion lies in crafting seamless and innovative web solutions, from front-end design to back-end functionality. In addition to my technical skills, I have a deep love for the arts, expressing myself through dance and painting, adding a creative touch to my work.

During my tenure at Publicis Sapient in Bangalore from Jan 2021 to Jan 2023, I developed REST APIs, integrated them with front-end systems, and designed user-friendly interfaces using JavaScript, ReactJS, and Redux. I also contributed to unit testing and automation testing efforts.

Prior to that, at Mindtree Ltd from July 2018 to Jan 2021, I focused on developing UI pages using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React JS, as well as managing web pages using AEM and Sightly. I also gained experience in working with Adobe Campaign and Target Tools.

I am actively seeking new job opportunities where I can leverage my skills, contribute meaningfully, and continue my professional growth. If you have an exciting opportunity that aligns with my expertise and experience, I would be delighted to explore it further. Please feel free to reach out; I am eager to connect and discuss potential collaborations.
